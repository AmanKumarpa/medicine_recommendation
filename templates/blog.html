🩺 Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App

Using Flask & Machine Learning

In the age of technology and information, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more critical than ever. With the increasing importance of remote healthcare solutions, we developed a symptom-based disease diagnosis web application using Flask and Machine Learning.

🚨 The Problem

People often experience symptoms and want quick answers about their health concerns. However, overlapping symptoms make it difficult to identify diseases accurately.

💡 The Solution

We created a web app where users input symptoms and receive predicted diseases using a trained Decision Tree model.

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter symptoms through an easy interface.
  2. ML Model: Decision Tree Classifier trained on symptoms dataset.
  3. Prediction: Instant disease prediction.
  4. Additional Info: Description, precautions, medication, diet & workout tips.

⭐ Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and easy to use.
  • Accurate Predictions: Powered by Machine Learning.
  • Complete Guidance: Includes diet, medicine & precautions.
  • Educational Content: Health awareness blogs.

🎯 Conclusion

This project combines healthcare and technology to provide quick, reliable, and accessible medical insights. It helps users understand their health better and promotes awareness in a modern digital way.
